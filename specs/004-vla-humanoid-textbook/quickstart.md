# Quickstart: VLA Humanoid Textbook Module 4

## Overview
Module 4 focuses on Vision-Language-Action (VLA) paradigm in humanoid robotics, explaining how language, vision, and action are unified in intelligent robots. This module teaches how large language models, speech recognition, and perception systems converge to enable humanoid robots to understand human intent and act autonomously in the physical world.

## Prerequisites
- Prior knowledge of ROS 2, simulation, and perception (as specified in module requirements)
- Basic understanding of robotics concepts
- Familiarity with Docusaurus documentation navigation
